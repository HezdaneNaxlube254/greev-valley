About Green Valley Academy

Building a Foundation for Lifelong Success

Our Story

Green Valley Academy was founded in 2009 with a clear mission: to provide quality, affordable education that nurtures the whole child. What started as a small primary school with just 50 students has grown into a thriving institution serving over 500 students from Grade 1 through Form 4.

Located along Ngong Road in Nairobi, we are proud to be a community-focused school that balances academic excellence with character development. Our experienced team of TSC-registered teachers works tirelessly to create a learning environment where students feel safe, supported, and inspired to reach their full potential.

Our Foundation

Our Mission

To provide quality, affordable education that develops academic excellence, strong character, and responsible citizenship in every student we serve.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution that produces well-rounded individuals who are academically competent, morally upright, and ready to contribute positively to society.

Core Values

  • Excellence in all we do
  • Integrity and honesty
  • Respect for everyone
  • Responsibility and accountability
  • Compassion and empathy

What Makes Us Different

Experienced Teachers

All our staff are TSC-registered teachers with years of experience and genuine passion for teaching.

Small Class Sizes

We maintain reasonable student-teacher ratios to ensure each child receives individual attention.

Modern Facilities

Well-equipped classrooms, science labs, library, computer room, and sports fields provide a complete learning experience.

Parent Involvement

We actively involve parents through regular meetings, progress reports, and open communication channels.

Character Development

Beyond academics, we focus on building strong moral character through guidance, counseling, and mentorship programs.

Community Focus

We teach our students to be responsible citizens who care about their community and environment.
