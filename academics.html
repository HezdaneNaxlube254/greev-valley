Our Curriculum

Green Valley Academy follows the Kenyan national curriculum as set by the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD). We offer both the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) for primary grades and the 8-4-4 system for upper primary and secondary, ensuring our students receive education aligned with national standards while developing critical thinking and practical skills.

Primary School (Grades 1-8)

Our primary program builds a strong foundation in core subjects while encouraging curiosity and love for learning.

Core Subjects (CBC - Grades 1-6)

  • English Language
  • Kiswahili
  • Mathematics
  • Science and Technology
  • Social Studies
  • Religious Education (CRE/IRE)
  • Creative Arts
  • Physical Education

8-4-4 Subjects (Standards 7-8)

  • English
  • Kiswahili
  • Mathematics
  • Science
  • Social Studies
  • Religious Education

Co-Curricular Activities

  • Music and Drama
  • Sports (Football, Volleyball, Athletics)
  • Science Club
  • Debate Club
  • Scouting and Girl Guides
  • Environmental Club

Secondary School (Forms 1-4)

Our secondary program prepares students for KCSE examinations while developing critical thinking and career readiness.

Compulsory Subjects

  • English
  • Kiswahili
  • Mathematics

Sciences

  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics

Humanities

  • History and Government
  • Geography
  • Business Studies
  • Religious Education

Optional Subjects

  • Computer Studies
  • Agriculture
  • Home Science
  • Art and Design
