Our Curriculum

Green Valley Academy follows the Kenyan national curriculum as set by the Kenya Institute of Curriculum Development (KICD). We offer both the 8-4-4 system and the Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC), ensuring our students receive education aligned with national standards while developing critical thinking and practical skills.

Primary School (Grade 1 - Standard 8)

Our primary program builds a strong foundation in core subjects while encouraging curiosity and love for learning.

Core Subjects

  • English Language
  • Kiswahili
  • Mathematics
  • Science and Technology
  • Social Studies
  • Religious Education (CRE/IRE)
  • Creative Arts and Physical Education

Co-Curricular Activities

  • Music and Drama
  • Sports (Football, Volleyball, Athletics)
  • Clubs and Societies
  • Scouting and Girl Guides

Secondary School (Form 1 - Form 4)

Our secondary program prepares students for KCSE examinations while developing critical thinking and career readiness.

Compulsory Subjects

  • English
  • Kiswahili
  • Mathematics

Sciences

  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics

Humanities

  • History and Government
  • Geography
  • Business Studies
  • Religious Education

Optional Subjects

  • Computer Studies
  • Agriculture
  • Home Science
  • Art and Design

Our Teaching Approach

Student-Centered Learning

We encourage active participation, questions, and discussions to make learning engaging and meaningful.

Continuous Assessment

Regular tests, assignments, and projects help us track progress and identify areas where students need support.

Remedial Classes

Extra coaching is provided for students who need additional help in specific subjects.

Exam Preparation

Structured revision programs and mock exams prepare students thoroughly for KCPE and KCSE.
