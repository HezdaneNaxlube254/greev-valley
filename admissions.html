Admission Requirements

Primary School (Grades 1-8)

  • Grade 1: Must be 6 years old by January 2025
  • Grades 2-8: Transfer students accepted with valid reports
  • Birth certificate (original + copy)
  • Immunization records
  • Previous school reports (Grades 2-8)
  • Transfer/leaving certificate (if applicable)
  • 2 passport photos
  • Parent/guardian ID copy

Secondary School (Forms 1-4)

  • Form 1: KCPE certificate & results slip
  • Forms 2-4: Valid school transfer documents
  • Birth certificate (original + copy)
  • KCPE certificate & result slip
  • Previous school reports
  • Leaving certificate from previous school
  • 2 passport photos
  • Parent/guardian ID copy
