How to Apply

Enrolling your child at Green Valley Academy is simple and straightforward. We welcome students throughout the year based on available spaces.

1

Contact Us

Reach out via phone, WhatsApp, or visit our campus to express interest in admission.

2

Visit the School

Schedule a tour to see our facilities, meet our staff, and learn more about our programs.

3

Submit Documents

Provide required documents including birth certificate, previous school reports, and transfer letter (if applicable).

4

Assessment

Students may take a simple placement assessment to help us determine the appropriate class level.

5

Enrollment

Complete the enrollment forms and pay the admission fee to secure your child's place.

Required Documents

  • Student's birth certificate (original and copy)
  • Parent/Guardian ID card (copy)
  • Recent passport-size photos (2 copies)
  • Previous school reports and leaving certificate
  • Medical records and immunization card
  • Transfer letter (for students joining from other schools)

Fee Structure

We believe quality education should be accessible to all families. Our fees are competitive and transparent, with no hidden charges.

What's Included:

  • Tuition for all subjects
  • Learning materials and textbooks
  • Co-curricular activities
  • Sports and games
  • Parent-teacher meetings

Additional Fees:

  • Admission fee (one-time payment): KES 5,000
  • Uniform: Available from approved vendors
  • Transport (optional): Varies by route

Payment Plans Available: We offer flexible payment options to help families manage school fees. Contact us to discuss arrangements.
